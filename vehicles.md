# Vehicles

## Overview

There are 23 vehicle types in Pump Theft Auto 2. Vehicles can be stolen from the streets, purchased at the Car Dealership, or found parked around the city. Each vehicle has unique stats that affect speed, acceleration, handling, and durability.

## Radio Stations

While driving, scroll the mouse wheel to switch between radio stations:

| Station | Genre |
|---------|-------|
| DEGEN RADIO | Electronic / Synth |
| BEAR MARKET ROCK FM | Rock |
| HODL FM | Chill / Lo-fi |
| LOS SANTOS DEL PUMP | Latin |
| RADIO OFF | Silence |

Each station has 3 original tracks that play continuously while driving.
